About Us

We are the Tomatoes and Orchard Producers Association of Nigeria (TOPAN), dedicated to advancing the interests of farmers and promoting sustainable agricultural practices across Nigeria.

Youth, Tomatoes and Agriculture in Nigeria

Half of the world’s population consists of young people, with 90% living in developing countries. Young people are innovative, interconnected, and adaptable. When empowered with entrepreneurial skills and confidence, they can significantly boost their countries’ economies.

In Nigeria, youth unemployment stands at 23% (approximately 39 million people) and continues to grow. Many women and young people avoid the agricultural sector due to perceived risks, costs, inefficiencies, and its labor-intensive nature.

To address these concerns and ensure food security, Nigeria must engage and empower youth by:

  • Addressing their aspirations and expectations for social mobility.
  • Replacing the ageing farming population (currently averaging 45-55 years old).
  • Increasing productivity to reduce dependence on food imports.

Vision

To provide cutting-edge farming techniques and services that enhance health and horticulture farming in Nigeria.

Mission

TOPAN’s mission is to:

  • Reduce hunger and malnutrition.
  • Combat climate change by reducing greenhouse gas emissions.
  • Promote sustainable livelihoods.
  • Enhance food security through advocacy, research, training, capacity development, and partnerships with farmers and stakeholders.

Goals

TOPAN's primary goal is to increase food security by ensuring access to safe, nutritious, and sufficient tomatoes and orchard products. This supports the Sustainable Development Goal of eradicating extreme poverty and hunger while promoting environmental sustainability.

Objectives:

  • Sustainable Production: Ensure stable, eco-friendly production that adapts to climate change.
  • Nutrition: Improve the nutritional benefits for consumers.
  • Technology Access: Provide modern technologies for optimal farming practices and capacity development.
  • Market Opportunities: Facilitate better market access and increased product availability.
  • Resource Access: Improve access to land, financing, and technology for farmers.
  • Image Enhancement: Enhance the perception of agriculture, especially among women and youth.
  • Collaboration: Encourage networking and collaboration among farmers.
