Meet Our Team

Oyeleke Bola Job

President/CEO

Oyeleke Bola Job has a degree in Master in Financial Economics (MFE) (In View) from the University of Abuja, a PGD in Business Administration from the University of Abuja, and a B.Sc in Economics from the University of Abuja. He also studied at the Federal College of Horticulture, Dadin Kowa, Gombe State.

Oyeleke Bola Job (COE/MD) advocates for long-term sustainability built on continuous capacity building in the communities. He envisions agricultural production as a means of alleviating extreme poverty and improving food security by strengthening employment opportunities, increasing farming knowledge, and improving the quality of life, making farming more rewarding and life in agricultural areas more attractive.

Mr Adetiloye Continental

Chief Executive Officer

Adetiloye Continental is a visionary leader and expert in agribusiness developmentwith over a decade of experience in tackling postharvest challenges across Africa.As the Founder of PostHarvest Africa (PHA), he has dedicated his career to reducingfood losses, improving supply chain efficiency, and fostering innovation in theagribusiness sector. Adetiloye has worked extensively with farmers, government agencies, private-sector stakeholders, and international organizations to develop scalable solutionsthat address critical issues in postharvest systems. Beyond his work with PHA, Adetiloye serves as the Executive Secretary of the Produce Export Development Alliance and co-founded Awesome Fresh, anagricultural logistics and cold chain company supporting over 1,500 foodprocessors. His mission is clear: to create efficient food systems that can sustainably feed over 900 million Africans daily while unlocking significant economic potential for the continent.
